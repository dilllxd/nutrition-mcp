Privacy & Terms

What we collect

When you register, we store your email address and a securely hashed password. When you use the service, we store your meal logs (description, meal type, calories, macros, notes, and timestamps).

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the nutrition tracking service. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or analytics.

Where it's stored

All data is stored in a self-hosted PostgreSQL database. Authentication is handled by the server itself using secure password hashing.

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by asking Claude to delete your account while connected to the Nutrition MCP server. This action is immediate and irreversible.

No warranty

This service is provided as-is, free of charge, with no guarantees of availability, accuracy, or fitness for any purpose. Use at your own risk.
